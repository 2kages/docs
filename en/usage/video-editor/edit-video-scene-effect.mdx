---
title: Edit Scene Effects
description: "Guide to editing scene effects with Vipro Studio"
---

Choosing scene effects will help make your video more dynamic.

- You can select scene effects from the Effects icon in the left corner menu.

- Then choose one of 5 available effects including: Zoom, Shake, Light leak, Mirror, Water ripple.

<img
  src='/en/images/usage/video-editor/scene-effects.jpeg'
  alt='Select scene effect'
  className='rounded-lg'
/>

- Additionally, you can choose to apply an effect to the entire video
